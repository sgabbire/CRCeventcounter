How are States and Local Governments Developing AI Policy?

February 25, 2025 | 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM EST

Register Now

About the Event

Join us for an insightful discussion on how state and local governments are approaching AI policy development. This event will explore current initiatives, challenges, and best practices in creating effective AI governance frameworks at the state and local levels.

Key topics include:

  • Current state of AI policy development across different states
  • Challenges in implementing AI governance at local levels
  • Best practices and lessons learned from early adopters
  • Balancing innovation with responsible AI use
  • Coordination between state and federal AI initiatives
