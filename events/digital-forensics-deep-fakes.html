Digital Forensics of Deep Fakes: From Research to Practice

November 12, 2024 | 1:00 PM - 3:30 PM EST

About the Event

Join us for a comprehensive exploration of the latest techniques in detecting and analyzing deep fake content. This session bridges the gap between academic research and practical applications in digital forensics, focusing on the challenges and solutions in combating sophisticated deep fake technologies.

Key topics include:

  • Current state of deep fake detection technologies
  • Advanced forensic analysis techniques
  • Machine learning approaches in fake content detection
  • Real-world case studies and practical applications
  • Future challenges and emerging threats

Featured Speakers

26-year FBI Computer Forensics Examiner
