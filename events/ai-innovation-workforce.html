About the Event

Join us for an enlightening discussion on the impact of AI on the workforce and the future of employment. This session will explore how AI is reshaping job markets, creating new opportunities, and the skills needed for the AI-driven economy.

Key topics include:

  • AI's impact on job markets and employment trends
  • Skills needed for the AI-driven economy
  • Workforce transition and adaptation strategies
  • AI-driven HR and recruitment innovations
  • Future of work in an AI-enhanced world
