About the Event

Join us for an in-depth discussion on privacy considerations in the context of the AI Executive Order. This session will explore the implications of AI development and deployment on individual privacy rights, data protection, and regulatory compliance.

Key topics include:

  • Analysis of privacy requirements in the AI Executive Order
  • Data protection frameworks and AI systems
  • Privacy-preserving AI techniques
  • Compliance strategies for organizations
  • Future of privacy in AI governance
