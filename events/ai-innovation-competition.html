About the Event

Join us for a comprehensive discussion on the international implications of the AI Executive Order, focusing on innovation and competition in the global AI landscape. This session will explore how the order impacts U.S. competitiveness while promoting responsible AI development.

Key topics include:

  • Global AI competition and U.S. positioning
  • Balancing innovation with security concerns
  • International collaboration frameworks
  • Impact on research and development
  • Strategic implications for U.S. AI leadership
